All about Synapse

How It Works

Our platform simplifies the data analysis process into three easy steps:

1

Upload Your Data

Simply upload your CSV files to our platform. We support datasets of all sizes.

2

Exploratory Data Analysis

Our platform automatically analyzes your dataset with visualizations like correlation matrices, mutual information heatmaps, pairplots, and feature-wise charts.

3

Automated Processing

Our platform automatically cleans, normalizes, and prepares your data for analysis, transforming it into an optimized format for real world use.

4

Bayesian Optimisation

Our platform efficiently fine-tunes machine learning models by automatically searching for the best hyperparameters, ensuring optimal performance.

5

Chat with the data

Use our conversational interface to ask questions about your data and receive accurate answers and insights instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to know programming to use Synapse?
Not at all! Our platform is designed for users of all technical levels. The conversational interface allows you to interact with your data using natural language. For advanced users, we also offer Python and R integration.
How do I customize the analysis steps?
You can customize every step of the analysis, from EDA to preprocessing and Bayesian optimization, including but not limited to feature selection methods, scaling techniques, test size ratio, scoring metrics, stopper types, and confusion matrix configurations.
What kind of visualizations are available in the EDA step?
We provide a range of visualizations including correlation matrices, mutual information heatmaps, pairplots, histograms, and pie charts, helping you explore your data in depth and uncover insights quickly.
How does Bayesian optimization improve my models?
Bayesian optimization helps fine-tune model hyperparameters to achieve the best performance. It uses a probabilistic model to intelligently search for the optimal settings, saving time and improving accuracy.
